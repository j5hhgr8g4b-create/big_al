# Big Al — App Screens v1

Mobile-first.

## Kitchen
Purpose: What should I do next?
Components: Restaurant name, Tonight's meal, Shopping status, Upcoming meals, Import/add recipe CTA, Basic suggestions.

## Cookbook
Purpose: What should I cook?
Components: Search, Recipe Books, Recently Added, All Recipes, Needs Review.

## Recipe Page
Purpose: Can I cook this?
Components: Image, Title, Creator, Time, Difficulty, Servings, Ingredients, Method, Source.

## Recipe Book Page
Purpose: Browse organised recipes.
Components: Cover image, Title, Description, Recipe list.

## Menu
Purpose: What are we eating?
Components: This Week, Next Week, Unplanned.

## Pantry / Shopping
Purpose: What do I need to buy?
Components: Shopping items, Checkbox, Quantity/unit, Manual add field.

## Cook Mode
Purpose: What do I do next?
Components: Recipe title, Step number, Instruction, Timer, Previous/Next, Ingredient list access.
