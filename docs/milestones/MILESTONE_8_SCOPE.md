# Milestone 8 Scope Guardrails

## Product principle

M8 is Basic Big Al, not a generic AI assistant.

Big Al should help a Restaurant member make better use of their saved Recipes, Menu, Shopping list and cooking history.

## Acceptable M8 behaviours

- Find saved Recipes.
- Recommend from saved Recipes using simple data signals.
- Explain why a Recipe is suggested.
- Surface recently cooked Recipes.
- Surface Recipes planned this week.
- Answer simple questions from stored Recipe data.
- Handle missing data honestly.

## Avoid during M8

- Open internet search.
- Scraping Recipes from the web.
- Nutritional or calorie tracking.
- Grocery pricing or store comparison.
- Inventory management.
- Public social feeds.
- Followers, likes or view counts.
- Claims Big Al cannot ground in stored app data.

## Future phase, not M8

- Advanced AI meal planning.
- Personalised adaptation engine.
- Ingredient substitution intelligence.
- Unit conversion and oven/hob preference adjustments.
- Import plagiarism/original creator detection.
- Rich community recipe ranking.
