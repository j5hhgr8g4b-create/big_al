# Big Al — Product Specification v1

## MVP

Big Al v1 is Restaurant → Cookbook → Menu → Shopping → Cook Mode, supported by basic contextual Big Al assistance.

## Navigation

Bottom tabs: Kitchen, Cookbook, Specials, Menu, Pantry.

## Kitchen

Purpose: What should I do next?

Shows: Tonight's meal, Shopping status, Upcoming meals, Import/add recipe action, Basic suggestions.

## Cookbook

The Cookbook is automatic. Every Restaurant has one Cookbook.

Contains imported recipes, created recipes, saved recipes, and adaptations.

## Recipe

Shows image, title, creator, source, time, servings, difficulty, ingredients, method, Cook, Add to Menu, and Edit.

## Recipe Books

Recipe Books organise recipes. They do not own recipes.

## Menu

This Week, Next Week, Unplanned, add recipe to day, people eating, servings estimate.

## Pantry

Shopping List only. Generate from planned Menu meals, add manual items, and tick off purchased items. No inventory management.

## Cook Mode

One step at a time, previous/next, ingredient list, timer support, mark cooked, cook again yes/no.

## Big Al

Search existing recipes, recommend existing recipes, answer basic recipe questions from stored recipe data.

Big Al must not automatically invent recipes.
