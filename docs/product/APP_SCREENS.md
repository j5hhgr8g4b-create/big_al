# Big Al — App Screens v1

Mobile-first.

## Kitchen
Restaurant name, Tonight's meal, Shopping status, Upcoming meals, Import/add recipe CTA, Basic suggestions.

## Cookbook
Search, Recipe Books, Recently Added, All Recipes, Needs Review.

## Recipe Page
Image, Title, Creator, Time, Difficulty, Servings, Ingredients, Method, Source.

## Recipe Book Page
Cover image, Title, Description, Recipe list.

## Menu
This Week planned meals, Next Week planned meals, Unplanned Recipe ideas, add existing Recipe to date, people eating, serving context.

## Pantry / Shopping
Generate from planned Menu meals, Shopping items, checkbox/tick state, quantity/unit, purchased section, manual add field, empty states.

## Cook Mode
Recipe title, step number, progress, large instruction, timer, previous/next, ingredient list access, mark cooked, cook again feedback.
