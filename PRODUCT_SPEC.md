# Big Al — Product Specification v1

## MVP
Big Al v1 is: Restaurant → Cookbook → Menu → Shopping → Cook Mode, supported by basic contextual Big Al assistance.

## Navigation
Bottom tabs: Kitchen, Cookbook, Specials, Menu, Pantry.

## Kitchen
Purpose: What should I do next?
Shows tonight's meal, shopping status, upcoming meals, import/add recipe action, and basic suggestions.

## Cookbook
Purpose: What should I cook?
The Cookbook is automatic. Every Restaurant has one Cookbook.
Contains imported recipes, created recipes, saved recipes, and adaptations.

## Recipe
Shows image, title, creator, source, time, servings, difficulty, ingredients, method, Cook, Add to Menu, and Edit.

## Recipe Books
Recipe Books organise recipes. They do not own recipes.
MVP: create, edit, add recipe, remove recipe, browse.

## Menu
Purpose: What are we eating?
MVP: This Week, Next Week, Unplanned, add recipe to day, people eating, servings estimate.

## Pantry
In MVP, Pantry means Shopping List only.
MVP: auto-generated list from Menu, ingredient consolidation, tick off items, manual add/remove.

## Cook Mode
Purpose: What do I do next?
MVP: one step at a time, previous/next, ingredient list, timer support, mark cooked, cook again yes/no.

## Basic Big Al
MVP: search existing recipes, recommend existing recipes, answer basic recipe questions from stored recipe data.
Big Al must not automatically invent recipes.
